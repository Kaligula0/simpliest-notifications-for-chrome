Use secure HTTPS instead of HTTP.
Check for notifications every [?].
Play Facebook notification sound if there are new notifications.
Click on the button always opens new tab. (Unchecked = onclick first try to find an opened Facebook tab, otherwise open a new tab.)


Note: The extension doesn't know your username and password. You have to stay logged in to Facebook even after closing Facebook tab/window, otherwise the extension will not know whose profile to watch.

The extension doesn't collect *any* data from your computer and web traffic. It only *passes* info from Facebook to you.
